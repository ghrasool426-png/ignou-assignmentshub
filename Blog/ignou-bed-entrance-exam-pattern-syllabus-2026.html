IGNOU B.Ed Entrance Test 2026

The IGNOU B.Ed Entrance Test 2026 is a mandatory examination for admission to the Bachelor of Education (B.Ed.) programme for the January 2026 session. Candidates must qualify this entrance test to be considered for admission.

What is IGNOU B.Ed Entrance Test?

The IGNOU B.Ed Entrance Test is a national-level examination conducted across India to shortlist eligible candidates for admission to the B.Ed. programme offered in Open and Distance Learning (ODL) mode. Admission is granted strictly on the basis of merit in the entrance test.

IGNOU B.Ed Entrance Test 2026 – Exam Pattern

  • Mode of Examination: Offline (Pen & Paper)
  • Type of Questions: Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs)
  • Medium: English and Hindi
  • Total Questions: 100
  • Total Marks: 100
  • Duration: 2 Hours

Section-Wise Question Distribution

Section Subject Area No. of Questions
Part A General English Comprehension 10
Part B General Hindi Comprehension 10
Part C Logical & Analytical Reasoning 20
Part D Educational & General Awareness 25
Part E Teaching-Learning & Subject Competency 35
Total 100

IGNOU B.Ed Entrance Test 2026 – Marking Scheme

  • Correct Answer: +1 mark
  • Negative Marking: No
  • Unattempted Questions: 0 marks
Important: Since there is no negative marking, candidates are advised to attempt all questions.

IGNOU B.Ed Entrance Test Syllabus 2026

1. General English Comprehension

  • Reading comprehension
  • Vocabulary
  • Basic grammar
  • Sentence formation

2. General Hindi Comprehension

  • अपठित गद्यांश
  • शब्दावली
  • व्याकरण
  • वाक्य संरचना

3. Logical & Analytical Reasoning

  • Number series
  • Coding–decoding
  • Blood relations
  • Analogy

4. Educational & General Awareness

  • Current affairs
  • Indian education system
  • Education policies
  • General knowledge

5. Teaching-Learning & Subject Competency

  • Child development
  • Teaching aptitude
  • Learning processes
  • Classroom management

Qualifying Criteria

IGNOU does not declare a fixed qualifying mark. Admission is based on entrance test score, merit ranking and availability of seats at Programme Study Centres.

Important Instructions

  • Carry admit card and valid photo ID
  • Use blue or black ballpoint pen only
  • Reach the examination centre before reporting time

Conclusion

The IGNOU B.Ed Entrance Test 2026 is the most important step for candidates seeking admission to the B.Ed. programme. Understanding the exam pattern and syllabus in advance will help candidates prepare effectively and improve their chances of selection.
