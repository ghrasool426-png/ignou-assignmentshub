About Us

Supporting IGNOU students with guidance and reference material

Who We Are

IGNOU Solved Assignments is an independent educational support platform created to help students enrolled in various IGNOU programmes such as MA, BA, BSc, BBA, and MSc. Our aim is to provide structured guidance and reference material to make academic preparation easier and more effective.

What We Do

We provide solved assignments and study material strictly for guidance and reference purposes. Our content helps students understand assignment patterns, improve answer presentation, and gain clarity on important topics.

Important Note for Students

We strictly advise students to prepare and write their assignments on their own. The material available on this website is meant only for guidance and reference. We do not promote, support, or encourage the submission of ready-made assignments to IGNOU or any other institution.

No Affiliation with IGNOU

This website is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or officially connected to Indira Gandhi National Open University (IGNOU). All names, logos, and trademarks related to IGNOU belong to their respective owners.

Our Mission

Our mission is to assist students by providing reliable academic guidance while maintaining ethical standards and respecting university rules. We aim to create a simple, transparent, and student-friendly platform that supports learning without compromising academic integrity.
