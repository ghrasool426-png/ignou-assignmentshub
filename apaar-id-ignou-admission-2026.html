APAAR ID Compulsory for IGNOU Admission 2026

Complete Guide for IGNOU Online & ODL Programme Applicants

IGNOU has introduced an important update for the January 2026 admission session. From now on, APAAR ID (Academic Bank of Credits ID) is mandatory for students applying to IGNOU Online and ODL programmes.

Without APAAR ID, students cannot submit the IGNOU admission form. This guide explains everything you need to know about APAAR ID in simple language.

What is APAAR ID?

APAAR ID (Automated Permanent Academic Account Registry) is a unique academic identity number introduced under the National Education Policy (NEP 2020).

It helps in digitally storing and tracking a student's academic records, credits, degrees and certificates across universities.

Why is APAAR ID Mandatory for IGNOU Admission?

  • To verify student academic records digitally
  • To avoid duplicate or fake admissions
  • To enable academic credit transfer
  • To comply with NEP 2020 guidelines

How to Create APAAR ID (Step-by-Step)

  1. Visit DigiLocker website: https://www.digilocker.gov.in
  2. Login using Aadhaar-linked mobile number
  3. Search for Academic Bank of Credits / APAAR ID
  4. Provide consent and generate APAAR ID
  5. Download or save APAAR ID for admission use

How to Use APAAR ID in IGNOU Admission Form?

While filling the IGNOU admission form, enter the exact APAAR ID number generated through DigiLocker. Any mismatch may cause application rejection.

What If You Do Not Have APAAR ID?

If you do not have APAAR ID:

  • You cannot submit IGNOU admission form
  • First generate APAAR ID
  • Then proceed with admission
