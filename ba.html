Frequently Asked Questions – IGNOU BA Assignments

Are these IGNOU BA solved assignments official?

No. All solved assignments available on this website are provided strictly for reference and academic guidance only. This website is not affiliated with IGNOU.

Can I submit these assignments directly to IGNOU?

No. Students are strongly advised to write assignments in their own words. Direct submission of copied content may lead to low marks or rejection.

Do you provide solved assignments for individual BA subjects?

Yes. BA solved assignments are available subject-wise and course-code wise. Each assignment can be unlocked individually via WhatsApp.

What is the price of one BA assignment?

Each BA subject assignment is available at a very affordable price. Pricing details are clearly mentioned with every subject card.

Which BA courses are covered on this page?

This page covers IGNOU BA Core Courses, Generic Electives, Discipline Specific and Honours courses based on current IGNOU structure.

Is following IGNOU assignment format important?

Yes. Students must strictly follow the IGNOU Assignment Format including front page, margins and handwriting rules to avoid mark deduction.

Where can I learn how to write IGNOU assignments properly?

You can read our complete step-by-step guide here: How to Write IGNOU Assignments .
