About IGNOU BA Assignments

The Bachelor of Arts (BA) programme offered by IGNOU follows a continuous evaluation system in which assignments play a crucial role. Each subject includes Tutor Marked Assignments (TMA) that must be submitted before appearing in the Term-End Examination.

IGNOU BA assignments are designed to assess conceptual understanding, analytical skills and clarity of written expression. Proper structure, relevance to questions and IGNOU-prescribed format are essential to score good internal marks.

How This BA Assignment Guidance Helps You

  • Clear, syllabus-oriented answers written in IGNOU academic style
  • Concept explanation with examples where required
  • Answer structure aligned with TMA evaluation pattern
  • Helps avoid common mistakes like irrelevant writing
Disclaimer: This website provides academic guidance and reference material only. Students must write and submit assignments in their own words. This website is not affiliated with Indira Gandhi National Open University (IGNOU).
