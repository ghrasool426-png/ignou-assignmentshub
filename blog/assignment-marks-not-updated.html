IGNOU Assignment Marks Not Updated?

Complete Step-by-Step Guide for IGNOU Students

Many IGNOU students become worried when their assignment marks are not updated on the official website. This issue is very common and usually gets resolved with time.

If your assignment marks are missing, showing "Not Found" or "Under Process", there is no need to panic. This guide explains exactly what you should do step by step.

First Step: Check Assignment Status on Official Website

Before taking any action, always check your assignment status on the official IGNOU assignment portal.

If the status shows:

  • Received
  • Under Process

üëâ This means your assignment has been successfully submitted and is currently under evaluation. In most cases, marks are updated automatically after evaluation.

If your status is Received or Under Process, the best option is to wait patiently.

Step 1: Contact Your Study Centre

If a long time has passed and marks are still not updated, your first contact point should be your Study Centre.

What you should do:

  • Visit or contact your Study Centre
  • Carry your assignment submission receipt
  • Confirm whether your assignment has been evaluated

üìå The receipt you received at the time of assignment submission is very important. Always keep it safe.

Step 2: Contact Your Regional Centre

If the Study Centre does not give a clear response, the next step is to contact your Regional Centre.

You should provide:

  • Assignment submission receipt
  • Enrollment number
  • Programme code
  • Course code
  • Study Centre details

You can contact the Regional Centre by visiting in person or by sending an email with scanned proof.

In many cases, assignment marks are updated after verification at the Regional Centre level.

Step 3: File an RTI (Last Option)

If you do not receive any response even after contacting both the Study Centre and Regional Centre, you may file an RTI (Right to Information).

RTI should include:

  • Copy of assignment submission receipt
  • Enrollment number
  • Programme and course details
  • Clear query regarding assignment evaluation
