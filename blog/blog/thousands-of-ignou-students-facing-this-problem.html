What Is This IGNOU Assignment Problem?

After submitting assignments, students expect marks to appear on their IGNOU grade card. Instead, many students see:

  • Status showing Received
  • Status showing Under Process
  • Blank assignment marks
  • Grade card not updated

This situation creates panic, especially when exams or results are near.

Why Are IGNOU Assignment Marks Not Updated?

1. Delay at IGNOU Study Centre Level

IGNOU assignments are evaluated manually at study centres. Due to staff shortage and heavy workload, evaluation often gets delayed.

2. Regional Centre Data Upload Delay

Even after evaluation, marks must be uploaded to the central IGNOU system. This process sometimes takes weeks.

3. Wrong or Incomplete Student Details

Small mistakes like incorrect enrollment number, course code or session year can prevent marks from reflecting.

4. Heavy Server Load During Result Season

During term-end result periods, IGNOU servers face heavy traffic, causing temporary delays.

Step-by-Step Solution to Get IGNOU Assignment Marks Updated

Step 1: Check Assignment Status Carefully

Always check status from the official IGNOU website. Make sure your details are correct.

Step 2: Wait for Normal Evaluation Time

Normally, marks are updated within 30‚Äì60 days after submission.

Step 3: Contact Your Study Centre

Visit your study centre with your assignment submission receipt. This receipt is very important.

Step 4: Contact Regional Centre

If study centre does not respond, email your regional centre with enrollment number, course code and receipt copy.

Step 5: Written Complaint

Submit a written application at the regional centre if required.

Step 6: RTI (Last Option)

RTI should be filed only if all other options fail.
