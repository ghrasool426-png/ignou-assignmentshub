How to Check IGNOU Admission Status Online

Students can check their IGNOU admission status online by following these steps:

  1. Visit the official IGNOU admission portal
  2. Click on Admission Status
  3. Enter your Enrollment Number / Control Number
  4. Select your programme and submit

Once submitted, your current admission status will appear on the screen.

Meaning of IGNOU Admission Status

1. Admission Confirmed

This means your admission is successful. You will soon receive your enrollment number and can submit assignments and appear in exams.

2. Admission Under Process

Your documents are under verification. This status is very common and usually resolves automatically.

3. Admission Pending

Admission pending may occur due to document issues, payment delay or heavy workload at IGNOU offices.

4. Documents Required / Rejected

If documents are incorrect or missing, IGNOU may ask you to upload corrected documents.

Why IGNOU Admission Status Is Not Updated?

  • High number of admission applications
  • Delay in document verification
  • Payment confirmation delay
  • Technical issues on IGNOU portal
  • Incomplete or unclear documents
