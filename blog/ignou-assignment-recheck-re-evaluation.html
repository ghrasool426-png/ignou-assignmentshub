IGNOU Assignment Re-check / Re-evaluation: Is It Possible?

One of the most common questions asked by IGNOU students is: ‚ÄúCan IGNOU assignment marks be re-checked or re-evaluated?‚Äù

If you feel that your assignment marks are low, incorrect, or missing, this detailed guide explains the official IGNOU rules and the correct solutions available to students.

Is IGNOU Assignment Re-check or Re-evaluation Allowed?

NO. As per official IGNOU academic rules, assignment re-check or re-evaluation is not permitted.

  • ‚ùå Assignment re-evaluation is not allowed
  • ‚ùå Re-totaling of assignment marks is not allowed
  • ‚úÖ Re-evaluation is allowed only for Term-End Exams (TEE)

This rule applies to all programmes including BA, MA, BSc, BBA, MSc, Diploma and Certificate courses.

Why Does IGNOU Not Allow Assignment Re-evaluation?

IGNOU assignments are evaluated at the study centre level as part of continuous internal assessment. Due to the decentralized evaluation system, IGNOU does not provide a formal re-check or re-evaluation option for assignments.

What Can Students Do If Assignment Marks Are Wrong?

Even though re-evaluation is not allowed, students still have practical solutions available.

1. Check Assignment Status Carefully

Many times, marks are evaluated but not updated on the portal. If the status shows ‚ÄúReceived‚Äù or ‚ÄúUnder Process‚Äù, marks may be updated later.

2. Contact Your Study Centre

If marks seem incorrect or missing:

  • Visit your Study Centre
  • Carry assignment submission receipt
  • Provide enrollment number and course code

Many issues are resolved due to data entry or upload errors at this level.

3. Approach the Regional Centre

If the study centre does not resolve the issue, submit a written application to your Regional Centre with proof.

4. File an RTI (Right to Information)

RTI is the most effective option when marks are delayed or missing for a long time. Through RTI, you can ask:

  • Whether the assignment was evaluated
  • Date of evaluation
  • Marks awarded

How to Avoid Low Assignment Marks in Future?

The best way to avoid problems is to submit well-structured assignments:

  • Follow correct IGNOU format
  • Use headings and subheadings
  • Write in your own words
  • Maintain word limit
