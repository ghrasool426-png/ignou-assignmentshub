IGNOU Assignment Re-check & Re-evaluation: Is It Possible?

Official IGNOU rules explained + practical solutions for students

One of the most common questions asked by IGNOU students is: “Can IGNOU assignment marks be re-checked or re-evaluated?” If your assignment marks are low, missing, or incorrect, this guide explains the official IGNOU rules and what you can actually do.

❌ IGNOU does NOT allow assignment re-check or re-evaluation.
✅ Re-evaluation is allowed ONLY for Term-End Exams (TEE).

Is IGNOU Assignment Re-check or Re-evaluation Allowed?

No. As per official IGNOU academic rules, assignment re-check, re-totaling, or re-evaluation is not permitted for any programme.

❌ Assignment re-evaluation not allowed
❌ Re-totaling of assignment marks not allowed
✅ Re-evaluation allowed only for TEE

This rule applies to all programmes including BA, MA, BSc, BBA, MSc, Diploma and Certificate courses.

Why Does IGNOU Not Allow Assignment Re-evaluation?

IGNOU assignments are evaluated at the study centre level as part of continuous internal assessment. Due to this decentralized system, IGNOU does not provide a formal re-check or re-evaluation facility for assignments.

What Can Students Do If Assignment Marks Are Wrong?

Step 1: Check Assignment Status Carefully
Many times marks are evaluated but not updated on the portal. If status shows Received or Under Process, wait for updates.
Step 2: Contact Your Study Centre
Visit or contact your study centre with assignment submission receipt. Errors often get resolved at centre level.
Step 3: Contact Regional Centre
If study centre does not respond, submit a written request to your Regional Centre.
Step 4: File an RTI (Right to Information)
RTI is the most effective option when marks are delayed or missing for long. You can ask:
  • Whether the assignment was evaluated
  • Date of evaluation
  • Marks awarded

Assignment vs Exam Re-evaluation (Comparison)

Component Re-evaluation Allowed
Assignment ❌ No
Term-End Exam (TEE) ✅ Yes
Grade Card Correction ✅ Yes
RTI ✅ Yes

How to Avoid Low Assignment Marks in Future?

  • Follow correct IGNOU assignment format
  • Use headings and subheadings
  • Write in your own words
  • Maintain word limit
