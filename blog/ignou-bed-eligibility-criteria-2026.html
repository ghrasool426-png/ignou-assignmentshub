IGNOU B.Ed Eligibility Criteria 2026

Confused about who can apply and who cannot apply for IGNOU B.Ed Admission 2026? Read the complete eligibility rules, minimum marks, NCTE requirement and age limit explained clearly.

Who Can Apply for IGNOU B.Ed 2026?

Candidates must fulfill academic qualification as well as professional eligibility conditions to apply for IGNOU B.Ed Admission 2026.

1. Minimum Educational Qualification

  • At least 50% marks in Bachelor’s Degree and/or Master’s Degree in:
    • Sciences
    • Social Sciences
    • Commerce
    • Humanities
  • OR Bachelor’s Degree in Engineering / Technology with 55% marks (Science & Mathematics specialization)

2. Professional Eligibility (Mandatory)

  • Trained in-service teachers in elementary education
  • Candidates who have completed an NCTE-recognized teacher education programme through face-to-face mode
Important: Candidates without NCTE-recognized qualification or proper teacher training are NOT eligible.

Who Cannot Apply for IGNOU B.Ed 2026?

  • Candidates with less than 50% marks in Graduation/Post-Graduation
  • Applicants who completed teacher education through online or distance mode
  • Fresh graduates with no teaching background
  • Candidates with non-NCTE recognized courses

Category-Wise Relaxation & Reservation

Category Relaxation
SC / ST / OBC (Non-Creamy Layer) 5% marks relaxation
Persons with Disabilities (PwD) 5% marks relaxation
EWS As per Central Government rules

Is There Any Age Limit?

No. There is no age bar for admission to the IGNOU B.Ed programme. Candidates of any age can apply if they meet eligibility conditions.

Common Eligibility Mistakes (Avoid These)

  • Applying without checking NCTE recognition
  • Assuming IGNOU B.Ed is open for all graduates
  • Ignoring minimum percentage requirements
  • Submitting application with incomplete documents
Final Tip: Meeting eligibility criteria does not guarantee admission. Final selection depends on entrance test score and merit ranking.
