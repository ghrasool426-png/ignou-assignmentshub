IGNOU Re-Registration for January 2026 Session: Complete Guide

The Indira Gandhi National Open University (IGNOU) has officially started the re-registration process for the January 2026 session for all ODL and Online programmes. Eligible students must complete the process online before the deadline.

Start Date: 1 December 2025
Last Date: 15 January 2026
Mode: Online (Samarth Portal)

Who Is Eligible for IGNOU Re-Registration 2026?

  • Students admitted in January 2025 (Annual programmes)
  • Students admitted in July 2025 (Semester programmes)
  • Learners moving to next year or semester
  • ODL and Online programme students
