IGNOU Assignment Marks Not Updated?

Complete Step-by-Step Solution for IGNOU Students

Thousands of IGNOU Students Face This Problem Every Year

Every year, thousands of students enrolled in Indira Gandhi National Open University (IGNOU) face a common issue â€” assignment marks not updated even after successful submission. If your assignment status shows Received or Under Process, this guide is for you.

Why IGNOU Assignment Marks Are Not Updated?

  • Delay in evaluation at study centre
  • Pending data upload at regional centre
  • Wrong course code or enrollment number
  • Heavy load during result declaration

Important Thing to Remember

If your assignment status shows Received or Under Process, your assignment is safe and under evaluation. There is no need to panic immediately.

Step-by-Step What You Should Do

Step 1: Check Official Assignment Status

Always check assignment status from the official IGNOU portal only.

Step 2: Contact Your Study Centre

Visit your study centre with your assignment submission receipt and confirm whether evaluation is completed.

Step 3: Contact Regional Centre

If marks are still not updated, email or submit a written application to your regional centre with receipt proof.

Step 4: RTI (Last Option)

If nothing works, students can file an RTI application along with assignment receipt. This is rarely required.
