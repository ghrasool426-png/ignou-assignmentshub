Thousands of IGNOU Students Face This Problem Every Year – Assignment Marks Not Updated

Every year, thousands of students enrolled in the Indira Gandhi National Open University (IGNOU) face a common and stressful issue — assignment marks not updated even after successful submission.

If you submitted your assignments on time, received the submission receipt, but your marks are still not showing on the IGNOU website, then don’t panic — you are not alone.

What Is the Problem Exactly?

After submitting assignments, students usually check their status online. Instead of marks, they see:

  • Status showing “Received”
  • Status showing “Under Process”
  • Blank assignment marks
  • Grade card not updated

In many cases, this status remains unchanged for weeks or even months, which creates panic among IGNOU students.

Why IGNOU Assignment Marks Are Not Updated?

1. Delay at Study Centre Level

Assignments are evaluated at study centres. Due to staff shortage, heavy workload and late checking, evaluation often gets delayed.

2. Delay in Regional Centre Upload

Even after evaluation, marks must be uploaded to the central IGNOU system. Technical issues and pending approvals can delay this process.

3. Incorrect Details

Any mistake in enrollment number, course code or session year can cause assignment marks to not reflect correctly.

4. Heavy Server Load

During result declaration periods, IGNOU servers experience heavy traffic, which can also delay updates.

Important:
If your assignment status shows “Received” or “Under Process”, it means your assignment is safe and under evaluation. There is no need to panic immediately.

Step-by-Step: What You Should Do

Step 1: Check Assignment Status Properly

Always check your assignment status from the official IGNOU website only. Ensure your enrollment number and programme details are correct.

Step 2: Wait for a Reasonable Time

IGNOU usually takes 30–60 days after submission to update assignment marks. If submission was recent, wait patiently.

Step 3: Contact Your Study Centre

If a long time has passed, visit your study centre with your assignment submission receipt. The receipt is very important — never lose it.

Step 4: Contact Regional Centre

If the study centre does not help, write an email to your Regional Centre with enrollment number, course code and receipt attached.

Step 5: File an RTI (Last Option)

If nothing works, students can file an RTI application. This option should be used only as a last resort.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does IGNOU take to update assignment marks?

Usually 1–2 months, sometimes longer during peak sessions.

Can result be declared without assignment marks?

No. Assignments are mandatory and results remain incomplete without them.

Do I need to resubmit assignments?

No, unless IGNOU officially asks you to do so.

Why Thousands of Students Face This Problem Every Year?

IGNOU has a very large number of learners. Manual evaluation, limited staff and centralized data upload make delays common — but most cases are resolved automatically.

Final Advice for IGNOU Students

  • Do not panic
  • Keep your submission receipt safe
  • Follow the correct step-by-step process
  • Trust only official IGNOU channels

Conclusion

Assignment marks not updated is one of the most common IGNOU problems. While the delay can be frustrating, it is usually temporary. By staying patient and informed, your marks will be updated.
