Why IGNOU Admission Gets Cancelled? Top Reasons & Easy Fixes

Every year, thousands of students apply for admission to Indira Gandhi National Open University (IGNOU). However, many learners later find that their IGNOU admission gets cancelled. In most cases, cancellation happens due to small and avoidable mistakes during the application process.

If you are planning to apply for IGNOU Admission 2026, this guide will help you understand the common reasons behind cancellation and how to fix them.

1. Incomplete or Incorrect Documents

One of the most common reasons for IGNOU admission cancellation is uploading incorrect or unclear documents.

  • Blurred or unreadable marksheets
  • Wrong qualification documents
  • Missing certificates
Fix: Upload clear scanned documents and double-check them before submission on the Samarth portal.

2. Not Meeting Eligibility Criteria

IGNOU strictly checks whether a student meets the eligibility criteria for a programme.

Fix: Read the programme eligibility carefully before applying.

3. Fee Payment Failure or Status Not Updated

Sometimes the fee is deducted but the payment status does not update.

Fix: Always save the payment receipt and check the Samarth portal regularly.

4. Ignoring the Correction Window

IGNOU provides a limited correction window. Ignoring it may lead to cancellation.

Fix: Check your registered email and dashboard frequently.

5. Uploading Fake or Invalid Documents

Uploading fake or edited documents leads to immediate cancellation.

Fix: Upload only original and genuine documents.

6. Delay in Verification by Regional Centre

Verification delays can also result in cancellation.

Fix: Contact your Regional Centre if verification is delayed.

7. Multiple Applications for the Same Programme

Submitting multiple applications for the same programme may confuse the system.

Fix: Submit only one application per programme.

What to Do If Your IGNOU Admission Is Cancelled?

  • Check the cancellation reason on the Samarth portal
  • Contact your Regional Centre immediately
  • Keep documents and fee receipts ready
  • Apply again in the next admission cycle
