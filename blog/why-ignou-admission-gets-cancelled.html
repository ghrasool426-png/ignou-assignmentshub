Why IGNOU Admission Gets Cancelled? Top Reasons & Easy Fixes

Every year, thousands of students apply for admission to Indira Gandhi National Open University (IGNOU). However, many learners later find that their IGNOU admission gets cancelled.

If you are planning to apply for IGNOU Admission 2026 , this guide will help you avoid common mistakes.

1. Incomplete or Incorrect Documents

  • Blurred or unreadable marksheets
  • Wrong qualification documents
  • Missing certificates
Fix: Upload clear scanned documents and double-check before submission.

2. Not Meeting Eligibility Criteria

Fix: Read programme eligibility carefully before applying.

3. Fee Payment Issues

Fix: Save payment receipt and verify status on Samarth portal.

What to Do If Admission Is Cancelled?

  • Check reason on Samarth portal
  • Contact Regional Centre
  • Apply again in next session if needed
