How to Write IGNOU Assignments (2025)

Complete Step-by-Step Guide for All IGNOU Courses

Writing IGNOU assignments correctly is very important to score good marks. This guide explains the complete process of writing IGNOU assignments in a simple and clear manner.

Step 1: Read the Question Carefully

Understand what the question is asking. Identify keywords such as discuss, explain, describe or analyse before writing the answer.

Step 2: Follow IGNOU Assignment Format

Use A4 size pages, write clearly with proper margins and include correct course code, enrolment number and study centre details on the front page.

Step 3: Write Answers in Your Own Words

IGNOU strictly discourages copied content. Answers should be written in your own words for better evaluation.

Step 4: Maintain Word Limit

Follow the word limit mentioned in the question paper. Writing too short or too long answers may affect marks.

Step 5: Proper Conclusion & References

End answers with a brief conclusion and mention references if required.

Important Note

All guidance provided on this website is strictly for educational and reference purposes only. Students must write assignments independently.
