How to Write IGNOU Assignments Properly (2025 Guide)

Step-by-step academic guidance for IGNOU students

Writing IGNOU assignments correctly is one of the most important factors for scoring good marks. Many students lose marks due to improper format, incorrect word limit, or copying content directly. This guide will help you understand the correct way to write IGNOU assignments in 2025.

What is an IGNOU Assignment?

IGNOU assignments are academic tasks given to students to evaluate their understanding of the subject. These assignments are mandatory and must be submitted before the term-end examination.

IGNOU Assignment Format

Follow the standard IGNOU assignment format:

  • Use A4 size paper
  • Write neatly in blue or black pen
  • Leave proper margins on all sides
  • Mention course code, subject name, and enrollment number
  • Number all answers properly

Word Limit and Language

Always follow the word limit mentioned in the assignment question. Answers should be written in simple and clear language. Avoid unnecessary long explanations.

Common Mistakes Students Should Avoid

  • Copy-pasting answers from the internet
  • Ignoring assignment instructions
  • Submitting incomplete answers
  • Poor handwriting and presentation

Tips to Score Better Marks in IGNOU Assignments

  • Understand the question before answering
  • Write answers in your own words
  • Use headings and subheadings
  • Revise before submission
