Common Mistakes in IGNOU Assignments

Reasons for Low Marks & How to Avoid Them

Many IGNOU students lose marks due to avoidable mistakes in assignments. Understanding these mistakes can help you score better.

1. Copy-Paste Content

Copying content from guides or the internet can lead to poor evaluation. Always write answers in your own words.

2. Ignoring Word Limit

Writing too short or too long answers can reduce marks. Follow the word limit mentioned in the question paper.

3. Poor Presentation

Untidy handwriting, missing margins and unclear answers affect the overall impression.
