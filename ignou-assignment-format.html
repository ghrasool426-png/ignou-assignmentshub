IGNOU Assignment Format (2025)

Front Page, Margins, Handwriting & Structure

Following the correct IGNOU assignment format is very important to score good marks. IGNOU follows specific academic rules while evaluating assignments.

IGNOU Assignment Front Page

The front page must include student name, enrollment number, course code, programme name, study centre and session.

Page Size & Margins

Use A4 size pages with proper left and right margins. Write neatly and clearly for better readability.

Handwriting Rules

Assignments should be handwritten unless otherwise mentioned. Avoid overwriting and use blue or black pen.

Important Note

This content is provided strictly for educational guidance only. Students must write assignments in their own words.
