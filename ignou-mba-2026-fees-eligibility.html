IGNOU MBA 2026

Fees, Eligibility, Admission Process & Career Scope

If you are planning to pursue an MBA in 2026 and are looking for a low-cost, flexible and government-recognised programme, then IGNOU MBA 2026 is one of the best options available in India.

The Indira Gandhi National Open University (IGNOU) offers an MBA programme specially designed for working professionals, fresh graduates and business owners who want career growth without leaving their job.

What is IGNOU MBA?

IGNOU MBA is a distance and online learning MBA programme approved by UGC and AICTE. It focuses on building strong foundations in management, finance, marketing, HR and operations.

The programme allows students to study at their own pace while continuing their professional or personal commitments.

IGNOU MBA Duration

  • Minimum Duration: 2 Years
  • Maximum Duration: 4 Years

This flexibility makes IGNOU MBA ideal for learners who cannot study full-time.

IGNOU MBA Eligibility Criteria 2026

  • Graduation in any discipline from a recognised university
  • Minimum 50% marks (45% for reserved categories)
  • No age limit
  • No mandatory work experience

Both fresh graduates and working professionals can apply without any restriction.

IGNOU conducts MBA exams twice a year:

Is IGNOU MBA Valid?

Yes. IGNOU MBA is 100% valid. The degree is recognised by UGC and AICTE and accepted by government organisations, private companies and for higher studies.
