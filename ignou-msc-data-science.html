IGNOU Introduces MSc in Data Science & Analytics

New Programme Details, Eligibility, Fees & Career Scope

Indira Gandhi National Open University (IGNOU) has launched a new MSc programme in Data Science and Analytics under its Open Distance Learning (ODL) mode. This programme is aimed at students who want to build strong analytical and computational skills for data-driven careers.

About MSc in Data Science & Analytics (IGNOU)

The MSc in Data Science and Analytics programme focuses on statistical methods, computational techniques and practical applications of data analysis. It is suitable for learners who want to work in software, analytics, research and academic fields.

Objectives of the Programme

  • Build strong foundation in statistics and data analysis
  • Develop computational and analytical problem-solving skills
  • Work with large and complex datasets
  • Prepare learners for data-oriented careers

Career Scope After MSc in Data Science

After completing this programme, students can explore careers such as:

  • Data Analyst
  • Data Scientist
  • Business Analyst
  • Research Assistant
  • Academic & Teaching roles

The programme is especially useful for learners aiming to work in technology, healthcare, business analytics and research sectors.
