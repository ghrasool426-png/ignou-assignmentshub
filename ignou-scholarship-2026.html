IGNOU Scholarship 2026

Eligibility, Amount, Documents & Application Process

The IGNOU Scholarship 2026 is a financial support initiative for students enrolled in Indira Gandhi National Open University programmes. This scholarship is aimed at helping learners from economically weaker and socially disadvantaged backgrounds so that financial problems do not become a barrier in higher education.

Who Can Apply for IGNOU Scholarship 2026?

  • Applicant must be an Indian citizen
  • Must be enrolled in any IGNOU programme (UG / PG / M.Phil / PhD)
  • Applicable for SC, ST, PwD, transgender candidates and economically weaker sections

Types of IGNOU Scholarships

IGNOU provides different scholarship options based on category and academic merit:

  • Special scholarships for women and minority students
  • Merit-based scholarships
  • National Fellowship for SC/ST/OBC candidates
  • Scholarships for Persons with Disabilities (PwD)

How to Apply for IGNOU Scholarship 2026?

The application process for IGNOU scholarship is offline only. Follow these steps carefully:

  1. Visit your nearest IGNOU Regional Centre
  2. Collect scholarship application form from State Social Welfare Office
  3. Fill in personal, academic and programme details
  4. Attach required documents
  5. Get the form verified by IGNOU Regional Director
  6. Submit the form to Social Welfare Department

Documents Required

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Previous academic marksheets
  • IGNOU admission confirmation letter
  • Income certificate
  • Caste / Category certificate (if applicable)
  • Disability certificate (for PwD candidates)

Benefits of IGNOU Scholarship

  • Reduces financial burden
  • Encourages higher education
  • Supports research scholars
  • Promotes inclusive education

Conclusion

The IGNOU Scholarship 2026 is a valuable opportunity for deserving students to continue their education without financial stress. Eligible candidates should apply on time and follow official guidelines carefully.
