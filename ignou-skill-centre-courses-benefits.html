IGNOU Skill Centre: Courses, Benefits & New Opportunities

Skill-Based Education & Job-Oriented Training by IGNOU

Indira Gandhi National Open University (IGNOU) has strengthened its focus on skill-based and employment-oriented education by launching and expanding IGNOU Skill Centres across India.

These centres aim to provide practical training, industry-relevant skills and vocational education to students pursuing online, distance and regular learning.

What is an IGNOU Skill Centre?

An IGNOU Skill Centre is a dedicated training hub that offers hands-on skill development programmes along with academic learning. The initiative aligns with the Skill India Mission and National Education Policy (NEP 2020).

Courses Offered at IGNOU Skill Centres

  • Information Technology & Computer Applications
  • Data Science & Analytics (select centres)
  • Healthcare & Paramedical Skills
  • Agriculture & Rural Development
  • Tourism & Hospitality Management
  • Fashion Design & Textile Skills
  • Entrepreneurship & Small Business Training
