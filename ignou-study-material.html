Why IGNOU Study Material is Important?

IGNOU study material plays a crucial role in assignment writing and exam preparation. Most assignment questions and term-end examination topics are directly based on official IGNOU books provided to students.

  • IGNOU assignments are evaluated based on official study material

  • Helps in understanding correct answer structure

How to Use IGNOU Study Material Effectively?

Many students make the mistake of memorizing study material instead of understanding it. Follow these tips for better results:

  1. Read each unit carefully before writing assignments
  2. Make short notes in your own words
  3. Use headings and subheadings in answers
  4. Relate answers directly to IGNOU book content
  5. Avoid copy-paste or direct reproduction

Relation Between Study Material & Assignments

IGNOU assignments are designed to test how well a student understands the official study material. Writing assignments without referring to IGNOU books often leads to low marks.
