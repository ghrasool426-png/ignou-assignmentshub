IGNOU 39th Convocation 2026

Registration • Eligibility • Convocation Fee • Degree by Post

The 39th Convocation of IGNOU is expected in February 2026. Eligible students will receive their Degree, Diploma or Certificate either by attending the convocation or through postal dispatch.

Key Highlights

Convocation: 39th
Month: February 2026
Sessions: Dec 2024 / June 2025
Options: Attend / Degree by Post

Eligibility

Students who completed their programme in:

  • December 2024 TEE
  • June 2025 TEE

are eligible to receive Degree, Diploma or Certificate.

Regional Centre Instructions

  • Attend at your registered Regional Centre only.
  • Certificates are not issued at other centres.
  • If not attended, certificate will be sent by post.
