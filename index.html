IGNOU 39th Convocation 2026

Registration • Eligibility • Convocation Fee • Degree by Post

The 39th Convocation of Indira Gandhi National Open University (IGNOU) is expected to be held in February 2026. Eligible students will be awarded their Degree, Diploma, or Certificate through this convocation.

IGNOU 39th Convocation 2026 – Key Highlights

  • Convocation Number: 39th
  • Expected Month: February 2026
  • Eligible Sessions: December 2024 & June 2025 TEE
  • Options: Attend Convocation or Receive Degree by Post

Eligibility for IGNOU 39th Convocation

Students who have successfully completed their programme in:

  • December 2024 Term-End Examination
  • June 2025 Term-End Examination

are eligible to receive Degree, Diploma, or Certificate.

Regional Centre Instructions

  • Students must attend the convocation at their registered Regional Centre only.
  • Degrees and certificates will not be issued at any other Regional Centre.
  • If convocation is not held at your centre, you may attend a nearby Regional Centre.
  • If not attended, the certificate will be sent by post.

Attend Convocation or Receive Degree by Post

During online registration, students can choose either:

  • Attend Convocation in Person
  • Receive Degree/Diploma/Certificate by Post

Important: Enter your correct and updated postal address.

IGNOU Convocation Fee Details

Programme Fee
PhD / Master / Bachelor / PG Diploma / Diploma ₹600/-
PG Certificate / Certificate Programmes ₹200/-

Accepted Online Payment Methods

  • Debit Card / Credit Card (Visa, Master, Rupay)
  • Net Banking

Print the acknowledgement after payment. It may take up to 48 hours to generate.
