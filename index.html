What Is IGNOU Assignments Hub?

IGNOU Assignments Hub is an independent academic guidance website created exclusively for students of Indira Gandhi National Open University (IGNOU).

We help learners understand assignment format, academic expectations and evaluation methods without promoting copying or unethical practices.

Academic Guidance Approach

This platform focuses on explaining how to approach Tutor Marked Assignments (TMAs), interpret questions correctly, organise answers with headings, follow word limits and understand IGNOU evaluation methods. The objective is conceptual clarity and ethical learning.

Programme-wise guidance, previous year question papers for exam orientation and official IGNOU updates are provided to support students throughout the academic cycle.
