About MA English 2nd Year Assignments

The second year of the IGNOU MA English (MEG) programme focuses on advanced literary studies, modern and postmodern literature, literary theory and research-oriented academic writing. Assignments at this level require critical thinking, textual analysis and use of theoretical perspectives.

IGNOU MA English 2nd Year assignments evaluate a student’s ability to interpret texts, apply literary theory, compare authors and movements, and present coherent academic arguments in a structured format.

IGNOU MA English 2nd Year Assignment Writing Tips

  • Use literary theories where relevant (Marxism, Feminism, Postcolonialism etc.)
  • Support arguments with textual references and quotations
  • Maintain clarity, coherence and academic tone
  • Avoid summary-only answers; include analysis and criticism
  • Conclude answers with critical evaluation
