About MA History 2nd Year Assignments

The second year of the IGNOU MA History (MAH) programme focuses on advanced historical analysis, modern and world history, historiography and research-based interpretation. Assignments at this stage require critical understanding, use of historical sources and analytical writing.

IGNOU MA History 2nd Year assignments test the student’s ability to evaluate historical processes, compare interpretations of historians and present structured academic arguments with clarity and originality.

IGNOU MA History 2nd Year Assignment Writing Tips

  • Use historiographical perspectives in answers
  • Support arguments with historical evidence and examples
  • Maintain chronological and thematic clarity
  • Avoid narrative-only answers; add critical analysis
  • Conclude with historical significance and evaluation
