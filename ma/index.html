What is IGNOU MA Assignment and Why is it Important?

In the IGNOU Master of Arts (MA) programme, assignments are an essential part of internal assessment. Students must submit subject-wise Tutor Marked Assignments (TMA) before appearing in the term-end examination.

IGNOU MA assignments carry significant weightage in the final result. Therefore, understanding the assignment pattern and writing approach is extremely important.

IGNOU MA Assignment Format and Writing Structure

  • Use A4 size sheets with proper margins
  • Mention course code, subject name and assignment code clearly
  • Write answers in simple, clear and structured language
  • Avoid copy-paste or reproduced content
  • Follow word limit and submission deadlines strictly
