About MA Political Science (MPS) Assignments

The Master of Arts in Political Science (MPS) offered by IGNOU focuses on political theory, Indian and Western political thought, comparative politics and international relations. Assignments form a crucial part of internal assessment and must be submitted before the term-end examination.

IGNOU MPS assignments require analytical writing, conceptual clarity and proper use of thinkers and theories. Students are advised to follow the IGNOU assignment format strictly and write answers in their own words.

IGNOU MPS Assignment Writing Tips

  • Begin answers with a brief introduction defining key concepts
  • Use thinkers, scholars and examples where required
  • Maintain logical flow and proper headings
  • Avoid unnecessary political opinions
  • End answers with a short conclusion
