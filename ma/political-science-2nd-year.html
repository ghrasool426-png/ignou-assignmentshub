About MA Political Science 2nd Year Assignments

The second year of the IGNOU MA Political Science (MPS) programme focuses on advanced areas such as comparative politics, international relations, public policy and political analysis. Assignments at this level require analytical depth, application of theories and structured academic writing.

IGNOU MPS 2nd Year assignments test the student’s ability to evaluate political systems, compare ideologies, analyze global and national political developments and present arguments logically.

IGNOU MA Political Science 2nd Year Assignment Writing Tips

  • Use political theories and concepts appropriately
  • Support answers with contemporary examples and case studies
  • Maintain analytical and balanced viewpoints
  • Avoid personal opinions; write academically
  • Conclude answers with critical evaluation
