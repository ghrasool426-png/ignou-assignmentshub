IGNOU 39th Convocation 2026

Registration • Eligibility • Convocation Fee • Degree by Post • Regional Centre Instructions

The 39th Convocation of Indira Gandhi National Open University (IGNOU) is expected to be conducted in February 2026. Eligible students will receive their Degree, Diploma, or Certificate either by attending the convocation or through postal dispatch.

IGNOU 39th Convocation 2026 – Key Highlights

  • Convocation Number: 39th
  • Expected Month: February 2026
  • Eligible Sessions: December 2024 & June 2025 TEE
  • Options: Attend Convocation or Degree by Post

Eligibility for IGNOU 39th Convocation

Students who have successfully completed their programme in:

  • December 2024 Term-End Examination
  • June 2025 Term-End Examination

are eligible to receive:

  • Degree
  • Diploma
  • Certificate

Regional Centre Instructions

  • Students must attend convocation at their registered Regional Centre.
  • Degrees will not be issued at any other Regional Centre.
  • If convocation is not held at your centre, you may attend a nearby one.
  • If not attended, the certificate will be sent by post.

Attend Convocation or Receive Degree by Post

During online registration, students can choose:

  • Attend Convocation in Person
  • Receive Degree/Diploma/Certificate by Post
Important: Enter your correct and updated postal address while registering.

If You Are Unable to Attend Convocation

  • If not invited or unable to attend, certificates will be sent by post.
  • Applicable to PG Certificate & Certificate Programmes also.
  • Certificates are dispatched by the concerned Regional Centre.

IGNOU 39th Convocation 2026 – Online Registration

Eligible students must apply through online mode only and pay the convocation fee digitally.

Convocation Fee Details

Programme Fee
PhD / MPhil / Master / Bachelor / PG Diploma / Diploma ₹600/-
PG Certificate / Certificate Programmes ₹200/-

Accepted Online Payment Methods

  • Debit Card / Credit Card (Visa, Master, Rupay)
  • Net Banking

Note: Print the acknowledgement after payment. It may take up to 48 hours to generate.
