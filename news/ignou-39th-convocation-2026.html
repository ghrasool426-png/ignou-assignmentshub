IGNOU 39th Convocation 2026: General Instructions, Eligibility & Registration

The 39th Convocation of Indira Gandhi National Open University (IGNOU) is likely to be held in February 2026. Eligible students will be awarded their Degree, Diploma, or Certificate through this convocation.

Students are advised to regularly visit the official IGNOU website for updates and notifications related to the convocation schedule.

IGNOU 39th Convocation 2026 – Key Highlights

  • Convocation Number: 39th
  • Expected Month: February 2026
  • Eligible Exam Sessions: December 2024 & June 2025 Term-End Examinations
  • Options Available: Attend Convocation or Receive Degree by Post

Eligibility for IGNOU 39th Convocation 2026

Students who have successfully completed their programme and passed all required examinations in December 2024 or June 2025 Term-End Examinations are eligible for:

  • Degree
  • Diploma
  • Certificate

Regional Centre Instructions

  • Students must attend the convocation at their registered Regional Centre only.
  • Degrees and certificates will not be issued at any other Regional Centre.
  • If convocation is not held at the registered Regional Centre, students may attend a nearby Regional Centre.
  • If not attended, the Degree/Diploma/Certificate will be sent by post.

Attend Convocation or Receive Degree by Post

During online registration, students will get the option to choose:

  • Attend Convocation in Person
  • Receive Degree/Diploma/Certificate by Post

Important: Students must fill their current and correct postal address while registering.

If You Are Unable to Attend Convocation

  • If a student is not invited or cannot attend even after paying the fee, the Degree/Diploma/Certificate will be sent by post.
  • This applies to PG Certificate and Certificate Programmes as well.
  • Certificates will be dispatched by the concerned Regional Centre after the convocation.

Students should remain in contact with their respective Regional Centre.

IGNOU 39th Convocation 2026 Online Registration

Eligible students must apply through Online Registration for 39th Convocation and pay the required fee through online mode only.

Convocation Fee Details

Programme Fee
Ph.D / M.Phil / Master Degree / Bachelor Degree / PG Diploma / Diploma ₹600/-
PG Certificate / Certificate Programmes (PGCAP, PGCAE, CWHM, CRUL, etc.) ₹200/-

Accepted Online Payment Methods

  • Debit Card / Credit Card (Master, Visa, Rupay – any bank)
  • Net Banking

After payment, students should print the acknowledgement. If it is not generated immediately, please wait for up to 48 hours.
