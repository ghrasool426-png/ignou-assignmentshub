About IGNOU B.Ed Programme

The Bachelor of Education (B.Ed.) programme of Indira Gandhi National Open University (IGNOU) is designed to prepare professionally qualified teachers for teaching at secondary and senior secondary levels. The programme focuses on integrating educational theory with practical teaching experience and reflective practices.

Programme Objectives

  • Understanding education in contemporary Indian society
  • Promoting inclusive education and learner diversity
  • Developing sensitivity towards language diversity
  • Understanding changes in curriculum and disciplinary knowledge
  • Addressing gender inequalities in education
  • Developing competencies for planning learning experiences
  • Using appropriate assessment and evaluation strategies
  • Strengthening school, community and learner connections
  • Integrating ICT in teaching-learning and school management
  • Enhancing professional teaching competencies
  • Providing internship-based school experience

Eligibility Criteria

  • Minimum 50% marks in Graduation or Post-Graduation
  • Completion of an NCTE-recognized teacher education programme through face-to-face mode

Admission Process

Admission to the IGNOU B.Ed programme is based on fulfillment of eligibility criteria and performance in the IGNOU B.Ed Entrance Test conducted across India.

Important: Appearing in the entrance test does not guarantee admission. Final admission is subject to merit rank and document verification.

Entrance Test Details

The entrance test will be conducted on the date notified in the official advertisement. Hall tickets will be uploaded on the official IGNOU website before the examination.

Seats & Examination Centres

  • Maximum intake: 50 seats per Programme Study Centre (PSC)
  • University reserves the right to change examination centres
