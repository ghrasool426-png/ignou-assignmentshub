IGNOU B.Ed Admission January 2026

Indira Gandhi National Open University (IGNOU) invites applications for the Bachelor of Education (B.Ed.) Programme for the January 2026 session. Admission will be conducted through an All-India Entrance Test.

About IGNOU B.Ed Programme

The Bachelor of Education (B.Ed.) programme of IGNOU is designed to prepare professionally trained teachers for teaching at the secondary and senior secondary levels. The programme integrates theoretical knowledge with practical teaching experience.

Programme Objectives

  • Understanding education in contemporary Indian society
  • Promoting inclusive education and learner diversity
  • Developing sensitivity towards language diversity
  • Understanding changes in school curriculum
  • Addressing gender inequalities in education
  • Developing competencies for planning learning experiences
  • Using appropriate assessment strategies
  • Strengthening school, community and learner connections
  • Integrating ICT in teaching-learning
  • Enhancing professional teaching competencies
  • Providing internship-based school experience

Eligibility Criteria

  • Minimum 50% marks in Graduation or Post-Graduation
  • Completion of an NCTE-recognized teacher education programme through face-to-face mode

Admission Process

Admission to the IGNOU B.Ed Programme is based on eligibility criteria and performance in the Entrance Test conducted across India.

Important: Appearing in the entrance test does not guarantee admission. Final admission is subject to merit ranking and document verification.

Entrance Test Details

The B.Ed Entrance Test will be conducted on the date notified in the official advertisement. Hall Tickets will be uploaded on the official IGNOU website.

Seats & Examination Centres

  • Maximum intake of 50 seats per Programme Study Centre (PSC)
  • The University reserves the right to change examination centres
