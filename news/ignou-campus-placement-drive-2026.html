IGNOU Campus Placement Drive 2026

Official job drive organized by the Campus Placement Cell (CPC), IGNOU on 27 January 2026 at IGNOU Campus, New Delhi. Open for IGNOU students and alumni across multiple sectors.

Placement Drive Overview

The Indira Gandhi National Open University (IGNOU), through its Campus Placement Cell (CPC), has announced a Campus Placement Drive for students and alumni. The initiative aims to connect IGNOU learners with employers from aviation, hospitality, sales, operations and business development sectors.

Date: Tuesday, 27 January 2026
Time: 10:00 AM onwards
Venue:
B. R. Ambedkar Convention Centre,
IGNOU Campus, Maidan Garhi,
New Delhi – 110068
Organized By:
Campus Placement Cell (CPC), IGNOU

Participating Companies & Sectors

1. Aviation & Airport Services

Company: Bird Flight Services

  • Customer Service Agent (CSA)
  • Customer Service Host (CSH)
  • Location: Delhi (Airport Operations)

2. Hospitality Sector

Company: YS Hospitality

  • Backend Office Support Staff
  • Office Admin / Graphic Designer
  • Sales & Operations Staff (Travel Background)

3. Business Development & Sales

Company: Pracxis Communications

  • Business Development Executive
  • Client acquisition and retention
  • Sales strategy and deal closures

Eligibility Criteria

  • IGNOU students and alumni
  • UG / PG / Diploma / MBA / BA candidates
  • Final-year or degree-pursuing students
  • Freshers and experienced candidates

  • Adaptability and willingness to learn
  • Time management
  • Basic digital literacy
