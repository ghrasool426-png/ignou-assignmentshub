IGNOU Launches New Skill Courses 2026

Job-oriented & skill-based programmes for students

Published: February 2026 | Category: IGNOU Update

Indira Gandhi National Open University (IGNOU) has announced new job-oriented skill courses for the 2026 admission cycle. These courses focus on employability, practical training and industry-relevant skills.

Key Highlights

  • Industry-focused skill programmes
  • Affordable fees
  • Flexible online & distance learning
  • Government-recognized certification

Who Can Apply?

Students from any stream who have passed minimum eligibility criteria can apply through the official IGNOU portal.
