IGNOU Launches New Job-Oriented Skill Courses for 2026 Admission Cycle

Published: February 2026 | Category: IGNOU Update

Indira Gandhi National Open University (IGNOU) has officially announced a range of new job-oriented skill courses for the 2026 admission cycle. These programmes aim to strengthen employability and bridge the gap between education and industry requirements.

The courses are designed with a focus on practical training, industry exposure and skill-based learning, making students job-ready in a competitive job market.

Focus on Employability and Practical Skills

IGNOU has aligned these skill-based programmes with current market demands. Learners will gain hands-on experience and real-world application skills useful for employment, self-employment and career growth.
