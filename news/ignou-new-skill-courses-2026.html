IGNOU Launches New Job-Oriented Skill Courses for 2026 Admission Cycle

Published: February 2026 | Category: IGNOU Update

Indira Gandhi National Open University (IGNOU) has officially announced a range of new job-oriented skill courses for the 2026 admission cycle, aiming to strengthen employability and bridge the gap between education and industry needs.

Focus on Employability and Practical Skills

These programmes emphasise hands-on training, industry exposure and real-world application, helping learners become job-ready in a competitive market.

Key Highlights of IGNOU Skill Courses 2026

  • Industry-focused curriculum
  • Affordable fee structure
  • Flexible learning (Online & Distance)
  • Government-recognised certification

Who Can Apply?

  • Students seeking job-oriented education
  • Working professionals upgrading skills
  • Aspiring entrepreneurs / self-employed learners
  • Learners needing flexible study options

Admission Process

Applications will open on the official IGNOU portal for the 2026 cycle. Check eligibility, course details and deadlines before applying.

Conclusion

IGNOU continues its mission of accessible, affordable and employment-focused education. These courses offer a strong pathway to improved employability.
