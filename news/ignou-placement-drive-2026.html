About the Placement Drive

Indira Gandhi National Open University (IGNOU) is organizing a Campus Placement Drive 2026 across multiple regional centres in Karnataka, Maharashtra, and Uttarakhand. This initiative aims to connect IGNOU students and alumni directly with recruiters and job opportunities.

Who Can Apply?

  • Any UG / PG / Diploma / MBA candidates
  • Pursuing or completed students
  • IGNOU Alumni

Placement Drive Schedule

Karnataka

  • 11 Feb 2026 – Bengaluru (Electronic City)
  • 13 Feb 2026 – Vijayapura (Bijapur)

Maharashtra

  • 10 Feb 2026 – Mumbai (New Panvel)
  • 11 Feb 2026 – Pune
  • 13 Feb 2026 – Nagpur

Uttarakhand

  • 12 Feb 2026 – Dehradun

Documents Required

  • Updated CV (2 copies)
  • Aadhaar / Voter ID / Passport
  • IGNOU ID Card
  • Passport-size photos
  • Original marksheets + photocopies
  • Experience certificate (if any)

Skills Employers Prefer

  • Communication
  • Teamwork
  • Creativity
  • Adaptability
  • Basic digital literacy
