IGNOU Extends Re-Registration Deadline for January 2026 Session Till 31 January

The Indira Gandhi National Open University (IGNOU) has officially announced the commencement of the re-registration process for the January 2026 academic session for all Open and Distance Learning (ODL) and Online programmes. According to the latest notification, the re-registration process began on 1 December 2025, and the university has now extended the last date to 31 January 2026.

This extension provides additional time to students who were unable to complete the process earlier due to academic, technical, or personal reasons.

Relief for Lakhs of IGNOU Students

The extended deadline comes as a major relief for lakhs of IGNOU learners in India and abroad. Re-registration is a mandatory requirement for existing students who wish to continue their studies in the next semester or academic year. Without successful re-registration, students will not be permitted to appear in examinations or access academic services for the January 2026 session.

Programmes Covered Under Re-Registration

The re-registration facility applies to all ODL and Online programmes, including:

  • Undergraduate programmes
  • Postgraduate programmes
  • Diploma courses
  • Certificate programmes

Students must re-register for the upcoming year or semester by paying the prescribed programme fee through the official IGNOU Samarth portal.

Reason Behind Deadline Extension

According to IGNOU officials, the decision to extend the deadline was taken after receiving multiple requests from students and regional centres. Many learners reported issues related to:

  • Online fee payment failures
  • Samarth portal access problems
  • Delay in declaration of previous semester results

How to Complete IGNOU Re-Registration

Steps to re-register:

  • Visit the official IGNOU Samarth portal
  • Login using enrollment number and programme details
  • Select courses for the January 2026 session
  • Pay the required programme fee online
  • Download and save the confirmation receipt

Students are advised to complete the process well before the deadline to avoid last-minute technical issues.

Important Clarification by IGNOU

IGNOU has clarified that re-registration is different from fresh admission. New applicants must apply through the admission portal, while existing students should use only the re-registration link.

Expert Advice for Students

Academic experts recommend that students regularly check official IGNOU notifications for updates related to re-registration, examinations, assignment submissions, and results. Failure to re-register within the extended deadline may lead to academic loss and delay in programme completion.

Conclusion

By extending the re-registration deadline till 31 January 2026, IGNOU has once again demonstrated its learner-centric approach. Students are encouraged to utilize the extended timeline and complete their re-registration at the earliest to ensure a smooth continuation of their academic journey.
