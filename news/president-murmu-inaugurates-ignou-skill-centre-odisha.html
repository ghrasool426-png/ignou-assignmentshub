President Murmu Inaugurates IGNOU Regional & Skill Centre in Odishaâ€™s Rairangpur

Published: IGNOU Education News

The Indira Gandhi National Open University (IGNOU) has taken a major step towards strengthening skill-based education in India. President of India, Droupadi Murmu, inaugurated a new IGNOU Regional and Skill Development Centre in Rairangpur, Odisha.

This initiative aims to expand access to quality education, vocational training and industry-relevant skills, especially for students from rural and tribal areas.

Objective of the New IGNOU Skill Centre

  • Promote skill-oriented and employment-focused education
  • Support Digital India & Skill India initiatives
  • Provide vocational, certificate and degree programmes
  • Improve access to education in remote regions

Focus on AI, Digital Skills & Emerging Technologies

The centre will emphasize modern and future-ready skills such as:

  • Artificial Intelligence (AI)
  • Data Science & Analytics
  • Digital Literacy
  • Entrepreneurship & vocational training

How Students Will Benefit

Students from Odisha and nearby regions will benefit through:

  • Local academic support and counselling
  • Reduced dependency on distant regional centres
  • Faster admission, assignment and exam-related services
  • Better access to online and ODL programmes
